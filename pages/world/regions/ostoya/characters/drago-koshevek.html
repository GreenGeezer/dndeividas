Grand Duke Drago Koshevek

Grand Duke Drago Koshevek is a Soman vampire and the supreme ruler of the Crimson Court. Ancient, calculating, and openly feared even among his peers, he oversees all provincial appointments and imperial policy across Ostoya. His word is law, and his authority is absolute.

Role in the Crimson Court

As the Grand Duke, Koshevek sits at the apex of Ostoyan power. The six provincial rulers answer directly to him, and failure to maintain control is met with swift and brutal removal. His influence extends into every corner of the empire through fear, manipulation, and centuries of accumulated leverage.
