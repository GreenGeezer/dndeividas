Raevo

← Back to Ostoya

Raevo occupies the northern reaches of Ostoya and has long been culturally distinct from the rest of the empire. Founded around prophetic visions and fortified against encroachment, the province has historically resisted both external conquest and internal authoritarianism. Since its secession, Raevo has reorganized into a magocratic republic—a beacon of resistance against the Crimson Court.

The Secession

Since its secession, Raevo has reorganized into a magocratic republic. Magical research unrestricted elsewhere in Etharis has become its primary defense against the Crimson Court. This has made Raevo both a beacon of resistance and a destabilizing influence within Ostoya.

Life in Raevo

Life in Raevo is harsh. Crop yields suffer under eternal dusk, necromantic energies prevent peaceful burial of the dead, and Valikan raids from the north devastate smaller settlements. Despite this, popular sentiment remains strongly anti-vampiric, and collaboration with the Court is widely regarded as treason.

Major Settlements

Castalore

Castalore stands as the province's heart: a fortress-city spiraling upward along a cliff face, designed for defense above all else. Governed by a Council of Mages, the city has never fallen to either Bürach or the Crimson Court. Internal political tension persists between pragmatic magocrats, the Order of Dawn, and religious or druidic factions.

Notable Individuals

Viscountess Nebüma Zeilgor

Strigoi agent of the Crimson Court. Assigned to reclaim Raevo. Shadow sorceress, infiltrator, and advisor to the Grand Duke on arcane matters.

Mina Taliesin, the Lady of Masks

First Minister of the Raevan Council of Mages. Master spymistress. Publicly enigmatic; privately committed to dismantling vampiric rule without inviting Bürach conquest.

General Artemios Dulka

Leader of the Order of Dawn. Veteran vampire hunter and former Soman noble, personally trained countless anti-vampire fighters.

Themes & Hooks

  • Magocratic Republic: Raevo represents the only functioning democracy in Ostoya, governed by mages and scholars.
  • Resistance Movement: The province is actively at war with the Crimson Court, creating opportunities for rebellion and espionage.
  • Harsh Environment: Eternal dusk, undead threats, and Valikan raids make survival a daily challenge.
  • Political Intrigue: Multiple factions vie for control—pragmatic mages, vampire hunters, druids, and infiltrators from the Court.
  • Hope & Defiance: Despite overwhelming odds, Raevo refuses to bow to vampiric tyranny.
