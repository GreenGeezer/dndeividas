Fallowheart is the fortress-city that dominates Estspine, both militarily and culturally. Home to the legendary Kaer Vena fortress, it has withstood centuries of sieges and now serves as a cornerstone of vampiric legitimacy under the rule of Viscount Langkirk Warlow.
