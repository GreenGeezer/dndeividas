Nov Ostoya

← Back to Soma

Nov Ostoya is the capital of the Ostoyan Empire and seat of the Crimson Court. Ruled by Grand Duke Drago Koshevek, the city is starkly divided between the desperate Low Streets and the elite Crimson Heights. Beneath it all lies the Necropolis—partially sealed, perpetually restless, and the source of the Darkfall that changed everything.

The Darkfall

The catastrophic event known as the Darkfall began here when seismic collapse revealed an ancient necropolis beneath the city. From this breach emerged vast undead forces, the sun ceased to rise over Ostoya, and the Crimson Court rose to power in the chaos that followed.
