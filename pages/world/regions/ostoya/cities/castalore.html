Castalore

← Back to Raevo

Castalore is the heart of Raevo—a fortress-city spiraling upward along a cliff face, designed for defense above all else. Governed by the Council of Mages, led by Mina Taliesin, the Lady of Masks, the city has never fallen to either Bürach or the Crimson Court.

The Magocratic Republic

Since Raevo's secession, Castalore has become the seat of a magocratic republic—the only functioning democracy in Ostoya. Internal political tension persists between pragmatic magocrats, the Order of Dawn, and religious or druidic factions.
