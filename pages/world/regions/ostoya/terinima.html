Terinima occupies the geographic and economic center of Ostoya. Nourished by the Great Arteriyan River, it once sustained vast farmland and open pasture. Though the Darkfall crippled traditional agriculture, Terinima remains the empire's primary source of food through adaptation rather than abundance.

The People of Terinima

The population consists largely of farmers, ranchers, hunters, and fishermen. Terinimans are insular, pragmatic, and resistant to ideological movements. Their priorities are survival and continuity, making the province comparatively easy to govern. Compliance is often purchased through selective mercy rather than force.

Major Settlements

Crowsbend

Settlements are dispersed and modest, favoring large family-held lands over dense urban centers. The notable exception is Crowsbend, a muddy but thriving trade town where goods, rumors, and contraband circulate freely. It serves as Ostoya's primary civilian crossroads.

Notable Individuals

Countess Augustine Vasinesca

Ecoukrylo overseer of Terinima. Advises the Court on food production. Known as the "Blood Sommelier," she selectively cultivates preferred vessels among the populace.

Themes & Hooks

  • Agricultural Survival: The province has adapted to grow food under eternal dusk through unconventional methods.
  • Rural Isolation: Dispersed settlements create opportunities for smuggling, hiding, and avoiding Court oversight.
  • Trade Crossroads: Crowsbend is a hub for merchants, spies, and those seeking information across Ostoya.
  • Pragmatic Compliance: Terinimans prioritize survival over ideology, making them unreliable allies but practical neighbors.
  • Blood Cultivation: Countess Vasinesca's selective breeding of "preferred vessels" creates disturbing social dynamics.
