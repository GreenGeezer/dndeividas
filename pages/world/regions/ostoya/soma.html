Soma is the political and economic core of the Ostoyan Empire. Fertile plains, coastal access, and trade dominance converge here. At its center stands Nov Ostoya, capital of the empire and seat of the Crimson Court.

Nov Ostoya - The Capital

Nov Ostoya is divided starkly between districts. The Low Streets are plagued by crime, addiction, and desperation, while the Crimson Heights house vampiric elites and collaborators. Beneath the city lies the Necropolis—partially sealed, perpetually restless.

The Darkfall's Legacy

Fissures from the Darkfall remain active throughout Soma. Undead incursions are frequent, and the populace lives under constant military oversight. Fear of monsters competes with fear of authority; often, the distinction is unclear.

The Necropolis

Beneath Nov Ostoya lies the ancient Necropolis—the source of the Darkfall. Though partially sealed by the Crimson Court, it remains active, spawning undead and leaking necromantic energy into the city above. What secrets and horrors remain buried in its depths are known only to the Court.

Notable Individuals

Grand Duke Drago Koshevek

Soman vampire, ruler of the Crimson Court. Ancient, calculating, and openly feared even among his peers. Oversees all provincial appointments and imperial policy. His word is law across all of Ostoya.

Countess Katalina Vulpescu

Influential courtier and spymaster. Controls intelligence networks across Ostoya. Maintains a public alliance with the Grand Duke while quietly stockpiling leverage against him and other Court members.

Themes & Hooks

  • Seat of Power: Soma is the heart of the Crimson Court's authority and the center of Ostoyan politics.
  • Urban Decay: Nov Ostoya's Low Streets are a cesspool of crime, corruption, and desperation.
  • The Necropolis: The ancient necropolis beneath the city continues to threaten the surface with undead incursions.
  • Class Division: The stark divide between the Crimson Heights and the Low Streets creates opportunities for intrigue.
  • Court Intrigue: The Crimson Court is a nest of immortal vipers, each plotting against the others.
  • Military Presence: Heavy military oversight keeps the populace in line but also creates opportunities for resistance.
