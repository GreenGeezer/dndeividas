Etharis

The Cradle of Despair and the Heart of Vandross

Etharis is the fractured core of Vandross — once a beacon of divine order, now a patchwork of kingdoms rotting beneath the weight of their own sins. The gods are silent, the noble houses bleed each other dry, and monsters walk openly beneath a sun that seems to grow dimmer each year.

A Continent Unraveling

From the icy coasts of Valika to the haunted ruins of Ostenwald, every corner of Etharis bears the mark of tragedy. Its people endure through grim pragmatism, black faith, and desperate hope — for none can remember a time when the dead stayed buried.

Themes of Play

  • Gothic Decay: Kingdoms crumble while dark miracles bloom.
  • Divine Silence: Faith persists though the heavens are empty.
  • Transformation: Power always extracts a price — blood, soul, or sanity.
  • Flickering Hope: Heroes stand as brief candles in the endless night.
